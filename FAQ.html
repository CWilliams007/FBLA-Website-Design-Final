Our Story: Helping Startups Get Started

In 2003 two friends and GCU Business Graduates, Leanna Murray and Indiana Ortega founded Accuracy Accounting in Phoenix, Arizona. Leanna and Indiana's parents had been a small business owners in Scottsdale, AZ since before either of them were born. Leanna and Indiana were passionate about helping small businesses grow. Since 2003 Accuracy Accounting has helped over 1200 small business owners in grow their companies.

What does our accounting firm do?

The IRS is constantly revising and changing tax codes. That is just one of many reasons why retaining the services of an outside accounting for both regular accounting/payroll and taxes is a great ideas. Your certified, professional accountant will be up to date on what can and can’t be written off as a tax deductible. They can also help you maximize the potential benefit of your purchases and expenses when you’re filing your taxes. Typically, purchases and expenses deemed necessary can be treated as tax deductible.

What does our bookkeeping service entail?

It includes accounts receivable, accounts payable, bank reconciliation, credit sales reconciliation, financial statement preparation and preparation of accounting write ups. Additional services offered include payroll, small business tax preparation and loan package preparation. We will also handle tasks that are required by the CPA for you.
